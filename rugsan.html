Rugsan

Indulge in Big Portions and Delicious Meats at Rugsan African Restaurant in Fargo

If you're in the mood for authentic African cuisine, head to Rugsan African Restaurant in Fargo. Located across the street from CVS, this cozy little eatery offers big portions of delicious food that will leave you full and satisfied.

The menu is filled with a variety of traditional African dishes, all made with the freshest ingredients and seasoned to perfection. The standout dish at Rugsan is their succulent grilled meats, cooked to perfection and bursting with flavor. The juicy chicken, tender beef, and delicious fish are just a few of the mouth-watering options that you can enjoy.

If you're feeling adventurous, why not try a little bit of everything and experience all the amazing flavors that Rugsan has to offer? So what are you waiting for? Head over to Rugsan African Restaurant in Fargo and treat yourself to a delicious meal filled with big portions and mouth-watering meats. You won't be disappointed!
